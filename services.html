With more than 10 years of experience in various companies, I offer my services as a freelancer in web accessibility, WordPress development and HTML/CSS development.
Whether you are a small or medium-sized company, a public authority or a web agency, I adapt to your needs according to your budget.

Web Accessibility

I accompany you from the beginning to the end of your project by giving advice and tools to each person according to their job in your company.
I carry out audits at each stage of the project (UX, Design, Front-end, Back-end) according to your needs.
I work in your organization to raise awareness of web accessibility and give your team the tools they need to build with accessibility in mind.

WordPress Development

From a graphic design or a theme of your choice, whether it is multilingual, multi-site or one-page, I take care of the creation and maintenance of your WordPress site.

Front-end Development (HTML/CSS)

With my favourite toolbox and based on your design, I produce a prototype that complies with w3C standards and is ready to be integrated.
I take particular care to respect web quality, performance and accessibility.
I use HTML5, CSS3, jQuery or Vanilla, Sass, Gulp technologies. I work with or without a framework (Bootstrap, Foundation) according to your needs.
I also ensure the backwards compatibility of browsers and responsive adaptation on mobiles and tablets.
