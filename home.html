About

Discovering a growing interest for the virtual creation while playing the videogame Heroes of Might and Magic, I had to inform myself about the trades of web and computing. At first glance I knew that I was made for it. But that was without taking account of my math teacher’s defiance when going in the IT way.

A few years later with becoming graduate of secondary school, I introduced myself in many computing schools. With my unfailing motivation to get in that course, I managed to enter the IGS at Lyon.

After more than 10 years in different web agencies, I'm working at Salt Mobile SA. My daily tools are HTML5, CSS3, Sass, Gulp and Screen readers. I’m very curious about new technologies and do techno watch every day. I’m really passioned by accessibility and web quality.
